Data Analytics Training Kemenperin

Pusat Industri Digital Indonesia (PIDI) 4.0 or The Indonesian Digital Industry Center 4.0 is part of the national priority program to achieve "Making Indonesia 4.0".

PIDI 4.0 is responsible for providing training to staff of the Ministry of Industry (Kementerian Perindustrian) to upgrade their knowledge of Industry 4.0. This includes Data Analytics Training which entails resolving organizational issues, designing analytical methodologies, and constructing dashboards for making choices.

PMD For Jakarta

Write an article with two colleagues about an analysis of the potential of Public Mobility Devices (PMD) to help people mobilize in first and last-mile phase based on Jakarta public transportation coverage. This project shows all the public transportation stops scraped from Trafi website and being processed to public transportation coverage using network analysis.

This project produces a web-based application that can be used to decide the best sites to build PMD stations.
